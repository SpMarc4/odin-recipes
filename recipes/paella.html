Paella

Description

Paella valenciana is the traditional paella of the Valencia region, believed to be the original recipe, and consists of Valencian rice, olive oil, rabbit, chicken, saffron or a substitute, tomato, ferradura or flat green bean, lima beans, salt and water. The dish is sometimes seasoned with whole rosemary branches.

Traditionally, the yellow color comes from saffron, but turmeric and Calendula can be used as substitutes. Artichoke hearts and stems may be used as seasonal ingredients. Most paella cooks use bomba rice, but a cultivar known as senia is also used in the Valencia region.

Ingredients

A very traditional Spanish paella, chicken, squid, mussels, shrimp, and peas. A paella pan is recommended for making and serving this dish.

  • Oil: 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • Onion:1 onion, chopped
  • Garlic:2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Red bell Pepper:1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • Chicken:2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into 1-inch cubes
  • Rice:1 (12 ounce) package uncooked Arborio rice
  • Chicken broth:5 cups chicken broth
  • White wine:½ cup white wine
  • Thyme:1 sprig fresh thyme
  • Saffron:1 pinch saffron
  • Salt:salt to taste
  • Black Pepper:ground black pepper to taste
  • Squid:2 squid, cleaned and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • Tomatoes:2 tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  • Peas:½ cup frozen green peas
  • Shrimp:12 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Mussels:1 pound mussels, cleaned and debearded
  • Leaf Parsley:¼ cup chopped Italian flat leaf parsley
  • Lemon:8 slices lemon, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a paella pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, garlic, and pepper; cook for a few minutes.
  3. Stir in 3 ½ cups stock, wine, thyme, and saffron; season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Check if the rice is cooked; add more stock if needed (up to 5 cups total).
  6. Stir in squid, tomatoes, and peas; cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Arrange prawns and mussels on top; cover with foil for 3-5 minutes.
  8. Remove foil, sprinkle with parsley, and serve with lemon wedges.
